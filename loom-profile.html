Your data on Loom

Under Loom's data transparency policy, you may request a copy of the data Altura Analytics holds about you. The export includes your posts, engagement history, activity log, connection graph, and derived scores. Processing takes up to 72 hours.

What the export contains
✓ All posts, including deleted posts retained by Altura (30-day retention after deletion)
✓ Likes, reactions, poll votes, and petition signatures
✓ Full activity log: session timestamps, search queries, hashtags clicked, posts viewed
✓ Connection graph: follows, blocks, mutes, and inferred social proximity scores
✓ Derived analytics: Loom Score history, reputation trajectory, engagement patterns
✓ Regional Health Index contribution data
✓ Emergency broadcast view logs and survey responses
✓ IP address logs and device fingerprints (72h rolling window)
✓ Compliance history and any reports filed about your account
— Direct messages are not included — these are peer-to-peer and not held by Altura
